About Me

Who am I?

Hi, I'm Tusar Gautam. I'm a multimedia student at Islington College who recently passed +2 from Tulsi Boarding School. I'm passionate about using technology and media to make a positive impact in the world.

What motivates me?

As a student, I often struggled to find comprehensive study materials for all subjects in the Nepali curriculum. This inspired me to create a notes website with the aim of helping other students like myself access high-quality notes and resources that can help them excel in their studies.

I'm also motivated by the desire to make education more accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. I believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to learn and grow, and I hope that my website can help facilitate that.

What challenges have I faced?

Creating this website has been a challenging but rewarding experience. One of the biggest challenges I faced was finding the time and resources to develop the website while balancing my studies and other commitments.

Another challenge was ensuring that the notes and resources on the website are accurate, comprehensive, and up-to-date. I spent a lot of time researching and reviewing different materials to make sure that I'm providing the best possible resources for my users.
